### Giving Instructions

Ramu said to the robot:

1. Take a cup
2. Add one spoon of tea leaves
3. Add sugar
4. Boil water
5. Pour water into the cup
6. Stir and serve

Now the robot worked perfectly.

They were **precise, exact, and unambiguous**.

### That Is Programming

**Programming is the art of telling a machine exactly what to do, step by step.**

* The **robot** is the computer
* The **instructions** are the program
* The **language** Ramu uses (Java, Python, C, etc.) is a programming language

Computers are powerful, but they are **very literal**.
